# 好坏型

## 首尾段

### 首段

- 话题同义改写
- 总观点
    - 最概括的写法：
        - 100%倾斜：I believe that this is a completely positive/negative trend.
        - 70%倾斜：I believe that this is generally a positive/negative trend.
        - 50%倾斜：I believe that this trend has
            - both positive and negative sides.
            - advantages and disadvantages.
            - benefits and drawbacks.
    - 具体一些：在某个层面是有有点或有缺点的
        - on a personal/societal/environmental/educational level
        - on the level of daily life

### 结尾段

同义改写分论点，不需要单独重复总观点

## 展开段

### 首句

第一个单词：
- 第一：First/Firstly/First of all
- 第二：Secondly/Second/Moreover/Furthermore
- 第三：Third/Thirdly/Lastly/At last

#### 1+2+1

每个展开段内的两个小分论点：
- 小一：To begin with/One concern/Specifically/Primarily/In the first place
- 小二：Additionally/In addition/Moreover/Another concern/Also/For another

回应题目指令：
- 一边倒态度：

    First, one positive aspect of X is ...  
    Moreover, X can also bring another benefit to ...

    - benefit用法：

        X can bring benefit to ...  
        X has the benefit for ...  
        X is beneficial for ...  
        X can benefit ...

- 中立态度：

    First, one positive aspect of X is ...  
    However, it is important to note/acknowledge the negative aspect of X.

    - 缺点写法：

        X has disadvantages/drawbacks/shortcomings related to ...  
        X can have negative/adverse effects/impacts on ...  
        X can negatively impact ...  
        X can be harmful/detrimental/damaging to ...

#### 1+3+1

- 中立态度

    First, one positive aspect of X is ...  
    Moreover, X can also bring a benefit to ...  
    However, it is important to acknowledge the negative aspect of X.

- 一边倒态度

    First, one positive aspect of X is ...  
    Moreover, X can bring a further benefit to ...  
    Lastly, yet another positive aspect of X is ...

#### 具体给出立场 ...部分内容

只用1-2个核心词来概括这个展开段的大方向

把一句话拆成两句写，将高级词汇写到扩充的第二句话中

### 论证

#### 因果关系

1. 因果逻辑连接词
- because 更强调原因
- since/as 更强调结果

2. Lead
- SVO. This can lead to + 结果

    前后两句权重一致

- SVO. This can lead to 直接结果, leading to/which can lead to + 补充结果

    主句权重更高

    两个lead造成重复，有两种改善方式
    - 使用连接词
        - Because SVO, SVO, leading to ...  
        - By doing sth, SVO, leading to ...
    - 同义词替换
        - 结果：SVO, resulting in sth.
        - 使得：SVO, allowing/enabling sb to do sth.
        - 促使：SVO, contributing to ...
        - 促进：SVO, encouraging/driving/motivating sb to do sth.
        - 起到重要作用：SVO, which plays an important role in doing sth.
        - 更具体的替换：
            - SVO, promoting/reduing ...
            - SVO, stengthening/weakening ...
            - SVO, making it easier/more difficult to do ...
        - 用factor替换：
            - A is a contributing factor to B
            - A is a factor that contributes to B

3. therefore、as a result、consequently

    用法：SVO. Therefore, SVO.

    引导完整句，总结性很强

4. thus/thereby、in turn

    附加在已经表示因果关系的句子上，进一步增加语气

5. which means

    用法：SVO, which means + 导致一个结果

# 观点型

## 展开段

### 首句

- 一边倒态度

    First, one argument for/in favor of X is ...  
    Second, another argument for/int favor of X is ...

- 中立态度

    First, one argument for/in favor of X is ...  
    However, one argument asgainst X is ...

argument使用过多，对argument替换：reason point

也可以对句子整体替换，例如：However, there are also arguments against X.

### 论证

#### 条件关系

if ... will ...

if的主要功能是：创建场景

if的同义替换：
- provided that
- on condition that

will较生硬、笃定，同义替换：
- tend to do
- be likely to do
- be prone to do

if引导的逻辑链的延长
- If SVO, SVO, leading to ...

    如果A发生，结果B发生，进而导致C发生

- 如果A发生，而不是A'，则B发生。相反，B不发生

语气更强的条件关系：只要
- only if
- as long as

程度最深的条件关系：无论
- no matter what/where/when + 完整句
- regardless of + 名词
- whether + 列举 / whether it be + 列举

#### 强调

especially, particularly

- 延伸成分

    A会做X动作 -> A,especially A1, 会做X动作

- 延伸完整句

    A会做X动作 -> A会做X动作。In particular, A1, (who/which)具有某种特点，更会去做X动作

## 首尾段

### 首段

同好坏型
- 话题同义改写
- 总观点

#### 总观点的写法

直接回应指令

I agree/disagree with this statement

对于辩证的题目，直接回答完全同意观点明确，但是如果不同意可能有很多种情况，表义不明

所以应该，针对两个部分的观点，分别给出对应的态度

先写不同意的部分，再写同意的部分

这样可以不用进行话题同义改写，因为改写部分与总观点结合了

### 结尾段

同好坏型

# 讨论型

## 展开段

一般分为三个展开段
- 对第一个预设观点的陈述
- 对第二个预设观点的陈述
- 我的看法

### 对预设观点的陈述段

必须使用第三人称

#### 首句

标准写法：
- First, some people argue that + 第一个预设观点的同义改写
    - To begin with, they believe that ...
    - For another, they also claim that ...
- However, other people argue that + 第二个预设观点的同义改写

变化写法：
- On the one hand, it could be argued by other people that ...
- On the other hand, a different perspective is held by others who believe that ...

#### 人称的使用

一个段落中人称需要统一
- 表示大众
    - 第一人称：we
    - 第三人称：(some/other) people、individuals、everyone
- 表示特定人群
    - 背语料

避免单数人称

### 论证

#### 列举

语法规则
- 2项并列：A and B
- 3项+并列： A, B, and C
- 等等：and other X (related) sth

可用来替换的句型
- such as
    - 如果修饰语是限定的，such as前没有标点
    - 如果修饰语是非限定的，such as前有逗号
- include
    - 作介词：SVO, including A, B, and C
    - 写完整句：SVO. This may/can include A, B, and C
- A and B are two ways

#### 举例

- 直接使用逗号或冒号代替for example
    - 冒号：
        - 表达：SVO：例子
        - 冒号的语法规则：
            - SVO: SVO.
            - SVO: Noun.
            - SVO: A, B, and C.
    - 逗号：
        - 表达：SVO, SVingO.
        - 独立主格：  
            e.g.：I love my mother, she working in the garden == she working in the garden, I love my mother
- imagine引导
    - Imagine + 名词, who/which ...
    - Imagine that + 句子； SVO
    - imagine的替换词
        - consider
        - suppose
        - take ... for example
- while引导  
    例子可以单独成句但没有完整到一个故事，用while连接两个例子

#### 让步

- although/while
    - while引导没有主次之分，although有
- in spite of/despite/notwithstanding
    - 介词，只能连接名词
    - 用法：Despite N, SVO.
    - 如果引导从句：Despite the fact that + 同位语从句，SVO.
- nonetheless/nevertheless
    - 需要在特定语境使用，总结性很强
- admittedly
    - 也需要在特殊语境使用，通常作为让步段的开头
    - 替代：we need to admit that ... / it is true that ... / there is no denying that ...

## 首尾段

### 首段

- 对话题的复述，用一句话概括两个预设观点之间的关系
    - There are two different perspectives regarding X.
    - There has been a conflict about X.
- 总观点
    - 倾向一个预设观点
        - I am more inclined to agree with A.
        - I have a stronger inclination towards concurring with A.
    - 我认为有解决方案
        - I believe there is a way to resolve this conflict.
        - I believe a resolution to this conflict can be achieved.
    - 认为两个观点都有道理
        - I hold the view that both opinion all have their merits.
        - From my perspective, there are benefits to be found in both opinions.

### 结尾段

总结分论点

## 我的看法段

### 倾向一个观点

- 首句
    - In my opinion, X can also bring benefits/harmful impacts to ...
    - In my opinion, there are other positive/negative impacts of X to consider.
- 第一句话之后  
    按常规方式写作，给出一个分支论点的展开

### 认为有解决方案

- 首句
    - In my opinion, while X may have negative effects, they can be overcome with solutions.
- 第一句话之后
    按常规方式写作，给出一个分支论点的展开

### 两个观点都有道理

将自己的观点穿插在预设观点段里

预设观点1：Some people think + 理由1. In my opinion, + 理由2.

预设观点1：Other people think + 理由1. From my perspective, + 理由2.

我的观点段与结尾段合二为一

在A和B之间寻求平衡：strike/achieve/keep/seek a balance between A and B
